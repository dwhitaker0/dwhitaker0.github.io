Dr. Darren Whitaker

Ph.D MChem MRSC

Senior Research Fellow, PMTC

Introduction

I received my Ph.D in analytical chemistry in 2014 from the University of Bradford. I am currently a Senior Research Fellow in the Pharmaceutical Manufacturing Technology Centre (PMTC) at the University of Limerick. Within the PMTC I work in the Chemometrics and Soft-Sensor research theme We seek to use mathematical, statistical and chemometric treatment of existing measurements as inferential sensors to predict end-points, qualitative attributes and visualise process evolution. Advanced PAT is also leveraged to provide rich data on molecular transformations and chemical reactions. Many of the approaches and techniques demonstrated can be applied universally accross multiple unit operations and processes

Previously I worked as a postdoctoral research associate at The Institute of Technology Sligo, Ireland. Where I was lead researcher on the Bio-PolyTec project. This was an FP7 EU funded project aiming to increase the commercial viability of Bioresorbable polymer implants by developing novel ways to monitor their production thereby reducing costs of manufacturing.

My main areas of research are based around electronic and vibrational spectroscopy, chemometric data analysis and soft-sensor development click here to see my full CV or here for a more in depth description of my present and past work.
