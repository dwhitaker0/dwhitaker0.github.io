Dr. Darren Whitaker

Ph.D MChem MRSC

Postdoctoral Research Associate, ITSligo

Introduction

I received my Ph.D in analytical chemistry in 2014 from the University of Bradford. I am currently a postdoctoral research associate at The Institute of Technology Sligo, Ireland. Here I am the lead researcher on the Bio-PolyTec project. This is an FP7 EU funded project aiming to increase the commercial viability of Bioresorbable polymer implants by developing novel ways to monitor their production thereby reducing costs of manufacturing. My main areas of research are based around electronic and vibrational spectroscopy, click here to see my full CV or here for a more in depth description of my present and past work.
